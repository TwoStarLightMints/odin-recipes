Pizzelle

Description

Pizzelle are a great dessert that are easy to make and last a very long time. This recipe will take you through step-by-step how to make approximately 2-3 dozen pizzelle. The only required equipment is a medium sized pizzelle press.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 eggs, room temperature
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3-1/4 cups all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, beat together the sugar and oil
  2. Add the eggs, salt, and extract, then beat together well
  3. Gradually stir in the flour until the mixture is smooth, the dough will be very soft and sticky
  4. Heat the pizzelle maker according to the manufacturer's instructions
  5. Wet your hands
  6. Take a scoop of dough the size of a walnut and roll between your hands to form a ball, or use a spoon to place the dough on the pizzelle baker
  7. Place the dough on the pizzelle maker cooking surface
  8. Close and clamp the lid and cook until golden brown, approximately 30 seconds
  9. Transfer pizzelle to paper towels to cool, and repeat with remaining dough
