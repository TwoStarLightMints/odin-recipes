Sugar Cookies

Description

This cookie recipe is of my favorites, because it is very simple and doesn't contain eggs or butter. You can either cut out the cookies with cookie cutters or a clean cup (depending on how fancy you want to be). These cookies will not spread very much, so you can put many cookies on a single baking sheet.

Ingredients

  • 1 stick All-vegetable shortening
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup seltzer water
  • 2 tablespoons of any milk alternative
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, and salt
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together the shortening and sugar
  3. Once creamed, pour in the egg, soy, and vanilla extract and wait till fully incorporated
  4. Slowly pour in the flour, baking powder, and salt mixture
  5. Pour contents onto a sheet of parchment paper and put in refrigerator for 1 hour
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  7. Take out the dough and roll it out on a counter top and cut out cookies using a bowl/cookie cutters
  8. Bake for 15 minutes or until golden brown on the bottom edges, and once done immediately take off the baking sheet
