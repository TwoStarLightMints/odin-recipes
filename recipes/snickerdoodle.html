Snickerdoodles

Description

Snickerdoodles are a timeless and excellent dessert for any occasion. These cookies will have slightly crisp edges and a soft inside. You will need a mechanical stirrer for this recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1-1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2-3/4 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 3 teaspoons cinnamon
  • A dash of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Mix butter, sugar, and eggs thoroughly in a large bowl
  3. Combine flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl
  4. Blend dry ingredients into butter mixture
  5. Get parchment paper and scoop the dough into the paper, chill for 15 minutes
  6. While dough is chilling mix cinnamon and sugar in a small bowl
  7. Once dough is done chilling, scoop approximately 1 inch globs of dough
  8. Coat the globs in the cinnamon/sugar mixture
  9. Cook the dough for approximately 15 minutes
